Implementing SSO using Google Authenticator(The Optional Task)

You can press on the Google Auth button at the top and log in with your Google account. After logging in, a button will appear that you can click to trigger an action on the backend. This action requires a token to validate it and ensure it comes from Google. With this token, you can access any endpoint or application without needing to log in again, effectively acting as SSO with a simple task.
